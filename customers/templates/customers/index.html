    Providing cutting-edge knowledge and state-of-the-art technologies and skills in the field of biotechnology and genetic engineering for sustainable development of society and environment.

    Mission

    • To provide quality education for providing competent graduates in biotechnology and genetic engineering to contribute in different sectors including agriculture, healthcare, industry and environment.
    • To facilitate the development of scientists, entrepreneurs, and policymakers towards nation building program.
    • To disseminate knowledge and skills for the betterment of the society and promote meaningful collaboration with academia, industry and research organization across the globe.

    Program Objectives

    To provide solution-based education with cutting edge knowledge in Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering in order to harness the latest technologies and methodologies for the graduates in the following field:

    • Microbial Biotechnology
    • Medical and Pharmaceutical Biotechnology
    • Industrial (bioprocess) Biotechnology
    • Agricultural (plant and animal) Biotechnology
    • Food Biotechnology
    • Environmental Biotechnology
    • Bioinformatics and System Biology

    Our Journey

Founding Chairman was Prof. Dr. M. Alauddin, a reputed professor and ex-Vice Chancellor of the Islamic University, Kushtia and Prof. Dr A.K.M. Nazmul Huda is currently serving as the Chairman of the department.

    Research Facilities

    01

    Laboratory of Microbiology

    Laboratory of Microbiology is a specialized facility where researchers study microorganisms, including bacteria, viruses, fungi, and protozoa. Microbiology encompasses various subfields, including medical microbiology, environmental microbiology, industrial microbiology, and microbial ecology. These laboratories are equipped with instruments and techniques for culturing, isolating, identifying, and characterizing microorganisms, as well as for studying their interactions with each other and with their environments.

    02

    Laboratory of Molecular Biology

    Laboratory of Molecular Biology is a specialized facility where researchers investigate the molecular mechanisms underlying various biological processes. Molecular biology is a branch of biology that focuses on the structure, function, and interactions of biomolecules, particularly nucleic acids and proteins, and how they regulate the genetic information within cells. These laboratories are equipped with state-of-the-art instruments and techniques for studying DNA, RNA, proteins, and their interactions.

    03

    Laboratory of Biochemistry

    Laboratory of Biochemistry is a specialized facility where researchers conduct experiments and investigations in the field of biochemistry. Biochemistry is the branch of science that explores the chemical processes and substances that occur within living organisms. These laboratories are equipped with instruments, tools, and expertise necessary for studying the structure, function, and interactions of biological molecules such as proteins, nucleic acids, lipids, and carbohydrates.

    04

    Laboratory of Plant Tissue Culture

Microscopes allow for detailed examination of rock and mineral samples, enabling geologists to identify their composition and study their microscopic features. Petrographic microscopes, in particular, facilitate the analysis of thin sections, thin slices of rocks or minerals mounted on glass slides.     06

    Laboratory of Bioinformatics

    Laboratory of Bioinformatics is a specialized facility dedicated to research and development in the field of bioinformatics. Bioinformatics is an interdisciplinary field that combines biology, computer science, mathematics, and statistics to analyze and interpret biological data, particularly data generated from high-throughput experimental techniques such as genomics, proteomics, and transcriptomics.

    07

    Laboratory of Environmental and Clinical Microbiology

    Laboratory of Environmental and Clinical Microbiology is a specialized facility that focuses on studying microorganisms in both clinical and environmental contexts. These laboratories play a crucial role in various fields, including public health, environmental science, medicine, and biotechnology.

    08

    Laboratory of Nanobiotechnology

    Laboratory of Nanobiotechnology is a specialized research facility focused on the intersection of nanotechnology and biotechnology. Nanobiotechnology involves the manipulation of materials and devices at the nanoscale (typically ranging from 1 to 100 nanometers) to develop innovative solutions for biological and medical applications.
